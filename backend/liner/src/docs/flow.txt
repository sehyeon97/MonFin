Anything capitalized is an Entity

1. Customer creates an account (new Customer)
2. Customer logs in
3. Customer selects Merchant (later add multiple merchants; atm only one)
4. Customer views Merchant's Products
5. Customer adds items to Cart
6. On checkout, select Card (payment method) and view list of Product(s) added to Cart
7. Payment processor compiles and creates Transaction Details, including the card token and cryptogram
8. Payment processor sends a post request to Customer's bank
(The communication process is written in Monfin docs)
9. Shows Transaction Result on the result page
10. Order is reflected on Merchant's and Customer's Order History pages respectively
11. Customer's purchased item is visually seen in Customer's Owned Items page
